nested">GLOBAL GARDEN

In partnership with Eastwood Community League, our Global Garden is your chance to get your hands dirty!

-bg">

nested">WHAT WE OFFER

Our Global Peace Garden provides many benefits including:

    ">
  • Meeting new people and working as part of a team for a common goal
  • The chance to get outdoors and be active
  • Beautification of the Eastwood neighbourhood
  • Places to share gardening knowledge and experience
  • Opportunity to harvest and take home vegetables
-bg">

nested">HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK

We hold periodic events between May and August starting with a spring clean-up and ending with the harvest. Anyone can drop in between events to weed the garden.

-bg">

nested">WHO CAN ACCESS THESE SERVICES?


    ">
  • Global Peace Garden is open to everyone.

nested">WHERE IS THE PROGRAM OFFERED?

Eastwood Community League, 11803 86 Street NW. The garden is located on 86 street NW just north of the skating rink.
