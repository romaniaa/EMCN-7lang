nested">GLOBAL COOKING

In our collective kitchen we come together to plan and prepare nutritious dishes from around the world. Have fun, socialize, share ideas and improve your cooking skills.

-bg">

nested">WHAT WE OFFER

Participants in Global Cooking benefit from:

    ">
  • Meeting new people and working as part of a team for a common goal
  • Sharing your skills and knowledge with others
  • A delicious meal
  • Recipes for new meals from different cultures
-bg">

nested">HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK

Global Cooking happens on Tuesday each week from 11am – 2 pm. Our winter/spring session runs February 7 to May 30. You do not need to bring anything with you, however you are welcome to bring a notepad and pen if you want to write down the recipe.

-bg">

nested">WHO CAN ACCESS THESE SERVICES?


    ">
  • Global Cooking is open to everyone and is free of charge.
-bg">

nested">WHERE IS THE PROGRAM OFFERED?

Alberta Avenue Community League, 9210-118 Avenue. Use the kitchen entrance on the Northwest side of the building.

-bg">

nested">HOW TO ACCESS SERVICES

No registration is required, just drop in and Sara, Community Wellness Facilitator, will be there to greet you.
