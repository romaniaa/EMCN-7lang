nested">GLOBAL VOICES CHOIR

Global Voices Choir is a welcoming place to learn and share songs from around the world.

-bg">

nested">WHAT WE OFFER

Members of the Global Voices Choir benefit from:

    ">
  • The chance to try out singing in different languages in a fun, non-judgemental setting
  • An opportunity to share your favorite songs with others
  • New friendships and supports
  • Participating in community events and festivals
-bg">

nested">HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK

Global Voices Choir happens each Thursday from 12 pm until 1 pm throughout the year except in the month of August. The Choir sings songs from around the world in various languages. Song books are provided as is a light lunch. Choir members can bring songs from their culture and teach the group. In addition to weekly gatherings, members of the choir have the option to join the Choir to perform at community events such as World Refugee Day and various arts and community festivals.
