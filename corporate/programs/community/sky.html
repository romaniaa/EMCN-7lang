nested">S.K.Y. CLUB (SUPPORT FOR KIDS AND YOUTH)

Sky Club offers newcomer children a range of activities and learning opportunities to give them the coping skills that will help them integrate into the Canadian school culture and form strong relationships.

-bg">

nested">WHAT WE OFFER

Children who are members of Sky Club benefit from:

    ">
  • Continuous positive adult support
  • Development of healthy relationships with peers and increased confidence
  • Tools and skills to help them to succeed in school
  • Support with homework
-bg">

nested">HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK

Throughout the school year, Sky Club runs each Saturday from 10:00 am until 2:00 pm. Newcomer children in grades 1 to 12 get together to do homework, literacy and numeracy activities, yoga, play leadership activities and workshops on life skills. The children are supported by tutors, youth support workers, parents and volunteers.

-bg">

nested">WHO CAN ACCESS THESE SERVICES?


    ">
  • Sky Club is open to all newcomer children enrolled in school in grades 1 through 12.
-bg">

nested">WHERE IS THE PROGRAM OFFERED?

Sky Club is offered at EMCN’s Eastwood site, 12023 – 81 Street NW

-bg">

nested">HOW TO ACCESS SERVICES

To register your child, contact the coordinator of Volunteer Services.
