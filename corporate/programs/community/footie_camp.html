nested">FOOTIE CAMP

Footie Camp is all about building confidence in kids — on and off the field. Footie Camp offers high-calibre week-long soccer camps with elite coaches for kids 8-12 years old.

-bg">

nested">WHAT WE OFFER

What Footie Camp offers:

    ">
  • Your child will work with Edmonton’s top coaches and athletes with 8:1 player coach ratio for personal attention to players
  • We put safety first
  • Players are grouped by skill level
  • No cancellations – we have an indoor space for bad weather days
-bg">

nested">HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK

Our elite coaches will help your child master the basics of soccer in weekly summer camps in July in Edmonton. Children will learn the core skills—like ball control, passing, shooting, dribbling—to become the best. They’ll learn those core fundamentals from the best in the country, and gain confidence and have fun. Lunch is provided for a small fee.

-bg">

nested">WHO CAN ACCESS THESE SERVICES?


    ">
  • Girls and boys between the ages of 8 and 12 of all skill levels.

nested">WHERE IS THE PROGRAM OFFERED?

Crestwood School, 9735 - 144 Street NW and indoors at Crestwood Curling Club, 14317 – 96 Avenue NW on rainy days

nested">HOW TO ACCESS SERVICES

Registration is online. There is a fee of $295 and proceeds from the camps are donated to the Free Footie program so kids who cannot afford the game can play for free.
