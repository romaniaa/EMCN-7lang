nested">SUCCESS BY SIX

Just like you, we want to see young children learn, grow, make friends and try new things. We also know that getting children involved in sports can be costly. Our Success by Six program provides low-income newcomer families with access to high-quality skating and swim/gym programming for their little ones.

-bg">

nested">WHAT WE OFFER

Participants in this program benefit from:

    ">
  • Swimming, ice skating and other physical literacy skills for young children
  • A chance for both parents and children to make new friends
  • A better understanding of recreational opportunities around the city
-bg">

nested">HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK

Pre-school Learn to Skate lessons are on Sunday afternoons for 30 minutes. Sessions are 10 weeks long and held once in the Fall and once in the Winter. Helmets and skates are provided. Swim and Gym lessons are on Saturday afternoons for two hours. The session lasts 8 weeks and is held in the Spring.

-bg">

nested">WHO CAN ACCESS THESE SERVICES?


    ">
  • Children aged 3 to 6 from low-income newcomer families. Parental supervision is required.
-bg">

nested">WHERE IS THE PROGRAM OFFERED?


    ">
  • Pre-school Learn to Skate is offered at Glengarry Arena, 13340 – 85 Street NW
  • Swim & Gym is offered at Castledowns YMCA, 11510 - 153 Avenue NW
-bg">

nested">HOW TO ACCESS SERVICES

To register for an upcoming session and to find out about session dates, contact Sara Assad, Community Wellness Facilitator.
