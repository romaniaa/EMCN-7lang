nested">WINTERGRATION

Make winter work to your advantage - resist the urge to stay indoors and instead try a new activity! Wintergration is a program for newcomers to Edmonton who would like to try out fun, outdoor winter activities.

-bg">

nested">WHAT WE OFFER

Wintergration provides many benefits including:

    ">
  • Meeting new people and having fun
  • The chance to get outdoors and be active
  • Opportunity to learn about and try popular Canadian activities
-bg">

nested">HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK

A variety of family events are held throughout the winter, such as tobogganing, cross-country and downhill skiing, skating, snowshoeing, river valley walks and curling. All equipment is provided so participants are able to have a hands-on winter recreation experience. While there is no cost to attend, participants must wear warm winter clothes.

-bg">

nested">WHO CAN ACCESS THESE SERVICES?


    ">
  • Wintergration activities are open to everyone, but some activities are not suitable for very young children.
-bg">

nested">WHERE IS THE PROGRAM OFFERED?

At various locations around the city.

-bg">

nested">HOW TO ACCESS SERVICES

Check our Facebook page for information on upcoming activities. Some activities are on a drop-in basis while others require registration in advance. To find out about the next Wintegration event, please contract Mischa Taylor.
