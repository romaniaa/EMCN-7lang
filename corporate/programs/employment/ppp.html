nested">PAYROLL PROFESSIONAL PROGRAM

The Payroll Professional Program is designed for learners interested in developing a strong foundation in the payroll field. Delivered in partnership with Northern Alberta Institute of Technology (NAIT), participants develop skills to succeed in fast-paced, challenging payroll environments.

-bg">

nested">WHAT WE OFFER

Graduates of Payroll Professional Program benefit from:

    ">
  • Opportunity to adapt and improve existing skills to Canadian standards
  • Enhance knowledge in Administration and Management, Clerical, Accounting, Personal service and Human Resources
  • Training in areas of business communications, time management, change management, networking and computers
  • A continuing education certificate and all required courses for their Payroll Compliance Practitioner (PCP) designation
  • Opportunity to participate in networking sessions with industry professionals
-bg">

nested">HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK

This program is delivered once yearly beginning in May to 14 eligible applicants. The program is full-time, for 11 months in duration and includes:

    ">
  • Four months in-class at the Edmonton Mennonite Centre for Newcomers developing English language workplace communication skills and computer skills
  • Six months in-class at NAIT completing 450 hours of coursework towards the Payroll Compliance Practitioner designation
  • One month of supported job search
-bg">

nested">WHO CAN ACCESS THESE SERVICES?

Candidates must fulfill the following eligibility requirements:

    ">
  • Minimum High School Diploma or General Educational Development (GED) Certificate
  • Unemployed or marginally employed
  • Canadian born and/or aboriginal graduate
  • Permanent resident or new Canadian Citizen; resident of Alberta
  • Language proficiency of Canadian Language Benchmark 5 or higher in all areas: reading, writing, speaking and listening
  • High motivation and commitment to completing the program
-bg">

nested">WHERE IS THE PROGRAM OFFERED?

Payroll Professional Program is offered at:

-bg">

nested">HOW TO ACCESS SERVICES

All interested applicants are required to write and pass a basic mathematics examination in order to determine application eligibility. The application deadline for the May program is February 15.
