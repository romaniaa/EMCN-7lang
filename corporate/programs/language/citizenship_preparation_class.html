nested">CITIZENSHIP PREPARATION CLASS

Citizenship Preparation Class is open to permanent residents who want to apply for Canadian citizenship.

-bg">

nested">WHAT WE OFFER

Participants in the program will benefit from:

    ">
  • Study materials and practice tests.
  • Language instruction to understand concepts and information needed to pass the citizenship test.
  • The chance to practice in a caring, non-judgemental environment.
-bg">

nested">HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK

Classes run in the fall, winter, spring and summer at EMCN Northeast. Each 11 week class follows the "Discover Canada" study guide.

-bg">

nested">WHO CAN ACCESS THESE SERVICES?

Applicants must meet the following eligibility requirements:

    ">
  • Permanent resident or convention refugee
  • Canadian Language Benchmark of 4 or higher.
