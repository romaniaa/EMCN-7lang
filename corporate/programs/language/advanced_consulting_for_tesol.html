nested">ADVANCED CONSULTING FOR EDUCATION (ACE) TESOL CERTIFICATE PROGRAM

ACE (Advanced Consulting for Education) is a nationally recognized provider of teacher training. EMCN collaborates with ACE deliver the TESOL Certificate Program for aspiring teachers of adult English language learners.

-bg">

nested">WHAT WE OFFER

Participants in the Certificate Program will benefit from:

    ">
  • An introductory English as a Second Language teacher training course that will provide you with the tools, strategies and knowledge to be successful as an English language teacher of adults.
  • The most current teaching methodologies, with a strong emphasis on practical teaching skills, and uses interactive classroom experiences as well as teaching demonstrations to enhance each participants learning experience
  • The ACE TESOL Certificate, with the 20-hour practicum, is recognized by TESL Canada for Professional Certificate Standard One
-bg">

nested">HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK

This program is delivered twice yearly to 10 participants on a first come, first served basis. The program is part-time, for 9 weeks in duration and includes:

    ">
  • Classroom learning: Mondays and Wednesdays 6:00 pm - 9:15 pm and Saturdays 9:00 am – 4:00 pm
  • A requirement to complete 6 hours of class room observation
  • A 20 hour practicum placement

For more information on the ACE TESOL Certificate course, visit ">www.aceducation.ca

-bg">

nested">WHO CAN ACCESS THESE SERVICES?

Applicants must meet the following eligibility requirements:

    ">
  • Strong proficiency in English. Applicants whose first language is not English must provide proof of proficiency:
    • TOEFL iBT Overall: 101
    • Listening: 23
    • Reading: 24
    • Speaking: 27
    • Writing: 27
    • IELTS Academic Overall 7, with a minimum of 7 in each skill area
    • Please check the TESL Canada website for a complete list of standardized proficiency tests and scores
  • Recommended Bachelor’s Degree (with any major) from an accredited postsecondary institution
  • Able to pay the course fee of $2000 plus a materials fee of $75 in full prior to commencement of the program
