">CAPACITY BUILDING FOR ETHNO-CULTURAL ORGANIZATIONS

The Empowered Communities program supports ethno-cultural communities using the Community Engagement approach, where the path to greater self-reliance must involve a process that empowers people and one that builds community and organizational capacity. We aim to engage communities from a set of values that is fundamentally strengths-based, promoting collective working, learning and reflecting, participation, political awareness and sustainable change.

-bg">

nested">WHAT WE OFFER

Ethno-cultural groups can access the following supports:

    ">
  • Assistance to leadership with identifying community needs through participatory needs assessment processes and engaging with the communities that they serve.
  • Information regarding grants, program support and implementation.
  • Assistance with program design, delivery and evaluation.
  • Training and workshops in board development, volunteer recruitment, strategic planning, community engagement and grant writing.
-bg">

nested">HOW DOES THE PROGRAM WORK

A member of the Empowered Communities team will meet with the members of the ethno-cultural group to jointly determine areas where the group can use support and areas where EMCN can assist. We connect the group to various community resources and/or work with the group over an agreed upon period of time to accomplish their goals. Ethno-cultural groups will also receive information about community events for their membership, and will be able to access periodic workshops and training sessions on different aspects of organizational development.
