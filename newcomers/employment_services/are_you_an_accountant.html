">WERE YOU AN ACCOUNTANT BACK HOME?

Accountants' Bridging Program

  1. ">WHO

    Accountants Bridging Program is open to:

      ">
    • Graduates of a degree program from an international institution with significant study and experience in the accounting field
    • Landed immigrants or new Canadian Citizens
    • Alberta Permanent Residents
    • Those with an English proficiency of CLBA 6 or higher
  2. ">WHAT

    ABP is an 11-month accounting program that includes:

      ">
    • 4 months (490 hours) in class training at EMCN to develop and enhance business communication and computer skills.
    • 6 months (550 hours) at NAIT for Canadian-specific accounting skills.
    • You will also receive 2 months of supported job search.
  3. ">WHERE

    Nova Plaza - ">8914 118 Avenue NW and NAIT - ">11762 106 Street NW

  4. ">WHEN

    We offer the program once per year starting every September.

  5. ">WHY

    Foreign-trained accountants graduate with a 95% employment rate in their field.

  6. ">HOW

    To learn more about registration, please call ">780 421 7400 or email ">abp@emcn.ab.ca
