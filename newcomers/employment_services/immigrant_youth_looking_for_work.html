">ARE YOU AN IMMIGRANT YOUTH LOOKING FOR EMPLOYMENT?

Immigrant Youth Employment Program

  1. ">WHO

    Our Immigrant Youth Employment Progam is for those aged 15-30 who are not working, not in school and not eligible for Employment Insurance.

  2. ">WHAT

    It is a 5 month funded career and life skills program with a 2 month paid in class component and a 3 month work experience to help you reach work and life goals.

  3. ">WHERE

    Nova Plaza - ">8914 118 Avenue NW

  4. ">WHEN

    There are different intakes throughout the year. Please call for more information.

  5. ">WHY

    To help immigrant youth with barriers gain employment and life skills and assist them in gaining meaningful employment or going back to school.

  6. ">HOW

    Apply today! Please call ">780 421 7400 or email ">iyep@emcn.ab.ca

  3. ">OÙ ?

    Nova Plaza - ">8914 118 Avenue NW

  4. ">QUAND ?

    Plusieurs dates d'admission sont réparties sur l'année entière. Veuillez téléphoner pour en savoir davantage.

    Aider les jeunes immigrants à surmonter leurs obstacles, à acquérir des compétences professionnelles et psychosociales, à trouver un emploi enrichissant ou à reprendre des études.

  1. ">कौन

    हमारा आप्रवासी युवा रोजगार कार्यक्रम, उन 15-30 वर्ष के लोगों के लिए है जो काम नहीं कर रहे हैं, स्कूल में नहीं हैं और रोज़गार बीमा के लिए पात्र नहीं हैं।
