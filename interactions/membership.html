">BECOME A MEMBER OF EMCN!

For over 35 years EMCN has walked along side newcomers as they learn to speak English, find a home for their family, settle their children into school, find a job, brave frigid winters, and make meaningful connections in the community. Our vision is a future where citizens walk with newcomers to bridge their transition into the community.

By becoming a member of EMCN, you’re signing up to be a champion of Social Justice, Responsibility, Compassion and Diversity! These are our core values, which guide the work we do with newcomers every day. If you're passionate about supporting refugees and empowering immigrants, then sign up today for opportunities to be involved and to make a difference. When you sign up, you’ll receive a welcome package of information, become a voting member at our AGM, receive EMCN updates and invitations to special events, and more!
